CORDOVA-CEBU LINK EXPRESSWAY

Nestled in the heart of a picturesque town, San Roque Parish emerges as a gem that captivates the hearts of tourists. Its unique blend of historical significance, cultural richness, and serene ambiance make it a standout destination for those seeking an unforgettable experience.
San Roque Parish boasts a rich history that dates back centuries. The architectural marvel of the parish church reflects the town's enduring heritage. Built with intricate details and timeless craftsmanship, the church stands as a testament to the community's resilience through various eras. Tourists are treated to a journey through time as they explore the well-preserved artifacts and stories that echo the past.
