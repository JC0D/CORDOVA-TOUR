SAN ROQUE PARISH CHURCH

TOURISM

Nestled in the heart of a picturesque town, San Roque Parish emerges as a gem that captivates the hearts of tourists. Its unique blend of historical significance, cultural richness, and serene ambiance make it a standout destination for those seeking an unforgettable experience.
San Roque Parish boasts a rich history that dates back centuries. The architectural marvel of the parish church reflects the town's enduring heritage. Built with intricate details and timeless craftsmanship, the church stands as a testament to the community's resilience through various eras. Tourists are treated to a journey through time as they explore the well-preserved artifacts and stories that echo the past.

HISTORY

It was on October 8,1864 that Cordova was made an independent town and parish by a diocesan decree under the advocation of San Roque. The parish has been administered for some time by Recollect fathers. The first church and convent were destroyed by the typhoon of November 25, 1877. Fr. Jose Baztan was one responsible for the reconstruction of the church made of mortar with pebbles and tabique pampango or wooden harigues. Fr. Baztan, a very holy priest who had always dreamed of volunteering for the China mission, was assassinated at the plaza of Opon by Cebuano insurgents on April 4, 1898. They threw his cadaver in the sea. Fr. Pedro Medina built the convent of masonry and wood in 1891. Both church and convent maintain their original form, except for a few restorations made recently. Under this church are many organizations and ministries that forms its structure. One of those ministries is the Youth ministry which named as San Roque Parish Youth (SRPY). San Roque Parish Youth (SRPY) is a ministry of San Roque Parish in Cordova Cebu. It composes of several youths gathered together as a servant of God. This ministry strengthens the relationship between the youth and our Almighty. It organizes activities which enhance the abilities and develops camaraderie among young people in Cordova. Its main goal is to lead other youths in a path which directs to the teachings of God. It encourages young men to become a follower of Jesus Christ. Lastly, it introduces them to encounter the presence of the Holy Spirit within us.

ENVIRONMENT

San Roque Parish Church in Cordova offers a peaceful retreat with its lush green surroundings. The tranquil environment creates a serene atmosphere, inviting visitors to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life. This church seamlessly blends history with nature, providing a unique charm that sets it apart. The welcoming community adds warmth to the experience, with friendly conversations and shared appreciation for the environment. During festivals, the surroundings come alive with cultural vibes, featuring traditional music, dance, and local cuisine. Preservation efforts by locals and authorities ensure the natural beauty remains unspoiled. San Roque Parish Church, therefore, stands not just as a historical site but as a living sanctuary where visitors can experience a harmonious blend of serenity and cultural richness.
