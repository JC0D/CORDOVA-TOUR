DAKIT-DAKIT

Dakit-Dakit is most visited at night. You might ask why? See for yourself. Dakit-Dakit has a good air refresher. Nature calls you, and the starry sky stares at you. It's not known to everyone yet because it's still getting renovated to become one of the most tourist spots in Cordova.

You can have a picnic here with your family, friends, partner, and even by yourself. You'll hear the sea breeze sing, and the moon reflects in the sea.

Dakit-Dakit can be located at "tumoy bantayan" or "tumoy catarman"
