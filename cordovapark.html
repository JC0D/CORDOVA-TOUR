CORDOVA PARK

The Cordova Park has been newly renovated with the help of Mayor Didoy Suan, and now Cordovanhons are proud of it. You can always take pictures here, although it is small, but it's worth it to visit this place. Its environment is clean, and it's also near the San Roque Parish Church, with food stands available.

You can also have a date here with your partner, stargazing when night comes, and drink milk tea. There's also a big Christmas tree standing here because it's the Christmas season. I'm waiting for what's coming next.

Cordova Park is located at the front gate of San Roque Parish Church.
