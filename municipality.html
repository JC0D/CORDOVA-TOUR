MUNICIPALITY OF CORDOVA

This is one of the oldest building in Cordova. I'll call it antique because of the museum here, and also the Rizal Statue. Everything about Cordova has evolved into a new Cordova, which is why many people visit Cordova for its wonderful change. The Municipality of Cordova holds a flag ceremony, and almost all government staff come to pay a visit to its wonderful municipal building.

Municipality of Cordova can be located at the center of Cordova.
