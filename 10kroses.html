10,000 ROSES

You can enjoy the view here when night comes, whether you're stargazing or staring at the artificial flowers made of waterproof fabric powered with LED lights. The sea breeze can also calm your heart.

Most people who come to 10,000 Roses are couples, talking about their future plans. 10,000 Roses also has a cafe, so they can eat there and enjoy their moment.
